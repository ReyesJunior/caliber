// Meet the Trainer

Section Header

I founded Caliber as a platform to promote a healthier and fitter world through fitness. Strength and conditioning are deeply embedded in the fabric of sports and, by their very nature, serve to sharpen and strengthen the mind and body.

Exercise is not just a deeply engaging discipline, but is a requirement to improve life's quality and richness. I want Caliber to help create a better world through fitness and exercise.

As a founder and CEO of CALIBER Strength and Conditioning, I am passionate about exercise and movement and work to make the world better through fitness. I embrace the philosophy that fitness helps shape and express a person's identity. To me, the nature of fitness is embedded in the pursuit of personal growth. I graduated UC Davis with a bachelor's degree in Exercise Biology, where I played as a collegiate athlete.
